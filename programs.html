Our Programs

Play-based, developmentally intentional care for every stage.

🌱 Infant Program

6 weeks – 12 months

Nurturing, responsive care in a calm environment. We follow each baby's natural rhythms for feeding, sleeping, and play.

  • Ratio: 1:4
  • Focus: Sensory exploration, bonding, tummy time, language exposure
  • Daily updates: Photos, feedings, naps, milestones via Brightwheel

🌿 Toddler Program

12 months – 30 months

Active, curiosity-driven learning for your explorer. Toddlers thrive on routine, choice, and safe independence.

  • Ratio: 1:5
  • Focus: Language development, gross motor, social-emotional skills, outdoor play
  • Curriculum: Emergent, play-based with intentional provocations

🌳 Preschool Program

30 months – 5 years

Preparing confident, curious learners for kindergarten and beyond — through play, projects, and community.

  • Ratio: 1:7
  • Focus: Early literacy, STEM exploration, creative arts, social collaboration
  • Kindergarten readiness: Embedded in daily activities, not drilled

Our Philosophy

Play is the work of childhood. Our curriculum is inspired by Reggio Emilia and emergent learning approaches — children are capable, curious, and natural researchers. We provide the environment, materials, and guidance; they provide the wonder.

Every child is seen. We observe, document, and celebrate each child's unique development. No two children follow the same path, and our approach honors that.

Indoor-outdoor, every day. Weather permitting, children play outside daily. Nature is our co-teacher — it builds resilience, creativity, and physical confidence.

Screens off. We are a screen-free environment. Children learn best through real materials, real relationships, and real play.
