Join Our Team

We're building a team of dedicated educators who believe childcare is a calling — not just a job.

Why Pomegranate House?

We believe that when educators feel valued, children thrive. That's why we're committed to:

  • Competitive wages — above market rate for our area
  • Small ratios — more support, less burnout
  • Professional development — paid training and growth opportunities
  • Respect & autonomy — your expertise matters here
  • Childcare benefit — discounted enrollment for team members' children
