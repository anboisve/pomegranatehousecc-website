Frequently Asked Questions

When are you opening?

We're targeting Spring 2027. Waitlist families will be the first to know our exact opening date.

What ages do you accept?

We'll serve children from 6 weeks through 5 years old (pre-K), with separate infant, toddler, and preschool classrooms.

Do you offer tours?

Yes! Once our facility is ready (late 2026), we'll invite waitlist families for tours and open houses. Sign up on our waitlist to be notified.

Do you accept childcare subsidies?

We plan to accept DSHS Working Connections childcare subsidies. Details will be confirmed closer to opening.

What's your curriculum approach?

Play-based and inspired by Reggio Emilia. We follow children's interests while ensuring developmental milestones are supported. Learn more about our programs.

Is there a waitlist fee?

No. Joining our waitlist is completely free with no obligation.
